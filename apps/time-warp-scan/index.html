About Time Warp Scan

Create surreal time warp scan effects with a single tap. A scan line sweeps across your camera view — and every pixel it touches freezes in place. That's the slit-scan effect: part live video, part frozen photograph, all in one frame. Use it on faces, movement, or anything in motion. What the app does: ✦ Slit-scan camera — a scan line moves across the frame, freezing pixels as it passes ✦ Direction control — run the scan horizontal or vertical ✦ Speed control — slow sweep for dramatic freeze, fast sweep for sharp capture ✦ Line color — match the scan line to your style ✦ Video recording — capture the full warp scan process as a video, not just a photo ✦ Gallery — browse all your warp scan photos and videos in one place ✦ Preview before saving — review every creation before sharing Built for the warp scan effect, the warp screen look, and the warp time camera style that turns ordinary moments into visual experiments. Works completely offline. No account needed. Create your own warp camera effects, warp filter shots, warp time scans — and share them anywhere.
