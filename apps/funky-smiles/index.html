About Funky Smiles

Give your screen a personality it deserves. Funky Smiles Live Wallpaper brings a curated collection of animated wallpapers and funny wallpapers designed for expressive, vibrant screen moments.

🎨 Browse & Discover

Explore smile themes, playful smiley face wallpaper designs, and creative fun wallpapers organized into categories. The explore screen and category filter let you find exactly the vibe you want — from funny backgrounds to bold funky wallpaper styles.

🖼️ Preview & Apply

Full-screen preview before you commit. Set any live wallpaper to your home screen, lock screen, or both — all in one tap. The moving wallpaper engine renders smooth animations without draining your battery.

⭐ Save Your Favorites

Bookmark the silly live wallpaper designs you love and return to them instantly. Your saved collection stays synced across sessions — no re-scrolling.

✨ Theme Variety

Whether you want smiley live wallpaper, evil smile wallpaper characters, or clean animated backgrounds, the library grows with regular updates. Set a smile live wallpaper that matches your mood — bright, bold, or playful.

All wallpaper designs are optimized for smooth performance on modern Android devices.
