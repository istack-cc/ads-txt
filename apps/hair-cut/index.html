About Hair Cut - Color Changer

Try on a new hairstyle or hair color in seconds with AI-powered previews that look natural on your own photo. Hair Cut - Color Changer lets you test short cuts, long styles, bangs, curls, and bold shades before you book a salon visit or make a big change.

✂️ AI hairstyle try-on

Upload a selfie and preview realistic haircut ideas instantly. The app maps your hairline, blends styles to your face shape, and helps you compare different looks without guesswork.

🎨 Hair color changer

Switch between blonde, brunette, black, red, copper, pastel, and vivid fashion colors in one tap. Adjust tone and intensity to see which shade suits your skin tone and lighting best.

📱 Fast before-you-dye decisions

Use it as a hairstyle simulator before a haircut, color treatment, wig purchase, or content shoot. Save multiple versions and compare styles side by side before committing.

✨ Built for quick experiments

No design skills needed. Just upload, test styles, save your favorites, and share them with friends or your stylist for feedback.

Hair Cut - Color Changer is a simple AI hairstyle try-on and hair color preview app for anyone who wants to explore new looks with less risk and more confidence.
