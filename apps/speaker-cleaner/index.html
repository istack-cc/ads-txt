About This App

Speaker Cleaner uses precise sound frequencies to remove water from your phone speaker and fix muffled audio in seconds. If your device sounds muffled after water damage or rain exposure, the water eject mode plays low-frequency tones that push liquid out of the speaker grills, restoring clear audio automatically. 🔊 Water Eject Mode The water eject function targets trapped moisture using optimized acoustic waves. The automatic cleaning cycle runs in under 60 seconds. Watch the real-time waveform visualizer as the app works — a feature no competing speaker cleaner offers. 💨 Dust Removal Mode High-frequency vibrations dislodge dust and debris from the audio mesh. The loudspeaker filter cycles through multiple tone profiles, and a circular progress timer tracks session duration at all times. 🎛️ Manual Frequency Control Advanced users can take full control: slide to any frequency level, choose between sine wave, square wave, or sawtooth wave — three distinct wave types for different cleaning approaches. A live frequency display shows the active tone in real time. 🎧 Headphone Cleaner The same vibration method works for earphones and headphone output. Remove water and dust from audio accessories without a separate app. 🔈 Speaker Sound Test After each session, verify your speaker health on the built-in sound test screen. Multiple test tone buttons confirm the output is clear and performing correctly. Key features: • Water eject mode — auto and manual, removes water using precise low-frequency tones • Dust removal mode — high-frequency vibration clears debris from speaker mesh • Manual frequency slider — full control over cleaning intensity • Wave type selector — sine, square, and sawtooth wave modes • Real-time waveform visualizer — see the cleaning signal live • Circular progress timer — tracks session duration • Speaker sound test — verify clarity after cleaning • Headphone cleaning support • Safe for all Android devices
