Whether you are driving, cycling, running, or travelling by truck or boat, this speedometer delivers accurate real-time speed readings, a full trip computer, and a reliable offline odometer, all in one clean interface.

⚡ Real-time speed display

The animated gauge dial shows your current speed in a large digital format. Switch freely between km/h, mph, knots, and m/s. A speed stats row tracks maximum speed, average speed, and current speed in a single glance. Set a speed limit and receive an instant visual overspeed warning the moment you cross it — useful for both safety and fuel efficiency.

📍 Speed tracker built for every journey

Every trip starts and ends on your terms. The built-in trip computer records start time, end time, duration, total distance, and average and maximum speed per journey. All trips save automatically to the trip history so your complete mileage record is always available. Pause, resume, or reset any trip without data loss. The car odometer screen accumulates GPS-precise distance across every drive — essential for delivery drivers, ride-share operators, and anyone who tracks mileage for expense reporting.

🚲 Speedometer bike and multi-activity tracking

The same GPS precision that powers car speed monitoring works equally well on two wheels. Cyclists get real-time pace, maximum speed, and trip distance on every ride. Runners and joggers track training pace across sessions. The app handles truck speedometer use on long hauls and doubles as a speed track reference when testing vehicle performance. Because it uses GPS rather than wheel sensors, accuracy is consistent across every activity and vehicle type.

🧭 Navigation tools and compass

A full digital compass screen displays real-time bearing and heading. An interactive map renders your current position and draws your route as you move — a live route tracker that keeps you oriented without a separate navigation app. A distance unit converter handles quick conversions between kilometres, miles, and nautical miles directly inside the app.

📶 Fully offline — no account required

The kph speed meter offline function uses satellite signal alone. Speed readings, trip recording, and compass bearing all work without mobile data — ideal for road trips in rural areas, international travel, and anywhere signal is limited. No account, no cloud sync, no subscription.

🔢 Speed units: kmh speedometer · mph app · knots · m/s

Switch between all four units at any time. The odometer app display updates automatically when you switch units, keeping your cumulative distance in your preferred measurement. Useful for international drivers moving between metric and imperial countries, and for marine use where knots are the standard.

Who uses this speedometer app ?
