About Color Picker

Ever wondered what color is this? Point your camera at any surface and instantly know the exact color — its name, HEX code, RGB values, HSL, and CMYK. Color Picker & Detector turns your phone into a precise color scanner app and color identifier, delivering professional color detection in real time without any guesswork.

🎨 Live Camera Color Detection

The built-in color detector app identifies any shade the instant your camera sees it. A precision crosshair locks onto your target and shows the color name, HEX, and RGB values as a live overlay. Whether matching paint on a wall or using it as a color finder for any shade in your environment, live color recognition handles it without delay. Similar shades and color suggestions appear alongside every detection.

📷 Pick Colors from Any Photo

Use the gallery image color picker to extract shades directly from any photo. A built-in magnifier gives pixel-level precision — so you always get the exact shade, not an approximation. Ideal for designers analyzing reference images or anyone needing to pull a precise color from image.

🔢 Full Color Format Converter

Every detected color shows a complete breakdown: HEX, RGB, HSL, and CMYK on one screen. The built-in color converter switches between formats instantly — no separate tool needed. Essential for developers needing rgb color picker values, designers working in CMYK, or artists controlling hue through HSL.

🎯 Color Matcher and Name Finder

The intelligent color matcher identifies the closest named color from a comprehensive database and shows a strip of harmonious, similar shades — perfect for building cohesive palettes. The similar colors finder is uniquely useful for interior designers, artists, and developers who need related tones at a glance.

🔍 Explore and Browse Colors

Browse the full color spectrum in a grid view, or filter by color family and category. Your complete color history is saved automatically — revisit every shade you've ever detected or picked. A palette generator view shows harmonious combinations based on any detected color.

✅ Built for Professionals and Everyday Users

Graphic designers extracting exact rgb cmyk codes · Web developers pulling precise color identifier app values · Interior designers matching paint to decor · Artists controlling pigment accuracy · Photographers analyzing tone — all in one lightweight, fast tool.
