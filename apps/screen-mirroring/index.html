About Screen Mirroring

Screen mirroring via Miracast — cast to TV wirelessly. DLNA, wireless display, smart tv cast, and HD screen mirror all in one app.

Mirror your Android to any TV or display instantly with our casting app. No cables, no setup. Wi-Fi Direct peer-to-peer means zero lag and total privacy — your screen goes directly to the TV without a router in between.

📡 Instant Device Discovery

Our network radar scans and detects available Smart TVs, wireless display adapters, and DLNA receivers in seconds. No pairing codes. No configuration. Just tap a device and the tv mirror session begins — wireless phone mirroring couldn't be simpler.

🖥️ True Miracast Screen Mirroring

Stream your full Android screen in real-time: apps, games, videos, browser, photos, presentations. Display mirroring via Miracast means what's on your phone appears on the big screen instantly. Our Miracast for android to TV engine automatically negotiates optimal stream quality for your wireless connection.

📺 TV Cast, Screencast & Media Sharing

The smart wireless tv casting mode handles every format: screencast your browser session, cast photos to TV, stream HD video without buffering. Works with any DLNA display, LED TV screen mirroring receiver, or Smart TV cast-compatible screen. Cast phone to TV — no account, no subscription, no barriers. Use it to cast to smart tv in any room.

🎬 HD Screen Mirror Quality Controls

Built-in bitrate and resolution controls give you full command over your wireless screen cast quality. Choose maximum HD screen mirroring fidelity or prioritize smooth performance for your network strength. TV mirroring at HD quality is a few taps away.

📊 Live Connection Health Monitoring

Real-time session tracker watches your wireless display connection status throughout every tv mirroring session. Screen cast health, stream bitrate, and connection stability all monitored live — so you always know your tv screen cast is running clean.
