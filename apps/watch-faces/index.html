About This App

Watch faces that finally look the way you want them to. Most watch faces are either overloaded with data or painfully generic. iStack Minimal Watch Faces gives your Wear OS smartwatch a face that matches your style — minimal, precise, and completely yours. Choose from 15 hand-crafted watch face designs, each built for clarity and character: Classic Minimal — Clean analog dial with simple hour markers. Timeless and distraction-free. Bauhaus — Geometric precision inspired by the Bauhaus design movement. Bold lines, clean form. Moon Phase — A lunar complication that tracks the moon cycle in real time on your wrist. Skeleton — Open-worked transparent face revealing the watch mechanism aesthetic beneath the dial. Tachymeter — Speed-measuring bezel design for a sporty, technical look with real precision. Tourbillon — A rotating complication-style face that brings fine watchmaking tradition to your wrist. Pixel — Android 12 Material You aesthetic with two Pixel-native rendering styles. Nordic — Scandinavian-inspired minimal design with clean whitespace and subtle typography. Arc Modern — Circular arc-based time display for a futuristic, minimal reading experience. Pulse — Animated heartbeat-style time display that makes every glance at your watch a moment. Slim Ring — Ultra-thin ring indicator for the purist who wants almost nothing on the dial. Field — Military field watch dial with high-contrast markers built for low-light reading. Digital Clean — Minimal digital readout with zero visual chrome and total clarity. Every watch face is fully customizable. Adjust colors, complications, and layout through the built-in watch face config screen. You are never locked into a default — every visual element can be changed to match your preference. Preview any design before applying it to your watch. The dedicated preview screen shows you exactly how the face will look in real use before you commit. Smart complications that matter: – Battery sync: display your phone's battery level directly on the watch face at all times – Notification count: see the number of pending notifications at a glance without lifting your phone – Watch connection indicator: know instantly when your wearable is paired and active Browse all 15 designs in the watch gallery screen before committing to one. Compare styles side by side. Try before you apply — every detail is visible in the preview before a single tap reaches your watch. Whether your style is clean and minimal, technically precise, boldly geometric, or warmly analog — there is a watch face here built specifically for it. Wear OS compatible. Works with Pixel Watch, Galaxy Watch, and all major Wear OS smartwatches.
