Precise Compass Navigation

The animated compass dial uses your phone's magnetometer to display accurate heading, bearing, and cardinal directions. Watch the needle respond in real time as you turn. The azimuth readout shows your exact angle, and the cardinal labels update continuously — north, south, east, and west are always clear on screen.

Camera Compass — See Direction in the Real World

Open the camera compass and see direction overlaid on your live camera feed. This augmented reality view helps you match the compass heading to what you actually see around you. GPS Location and Coordinates

View your current GPS coordinates with latitude and longitude displayed clearly. The location screen shows your exact position, useful for sharing your spot with others, logging hiking waypoints, or confirming your position on a trail. The GPS compass feature works alongside sensor data for enhanced directional accuracy.

Bubble Level Tool

A built-in spirit level uses the accelerometer to measure surface angle with precision. Use it to check if a surface is flat, hang a picture straight, or level camping equipment. The level meter shows angle calculations in real time — a handy utility tool that saves you from carrying extra gear.

Weather Conditions

Check current weather conditions directly inside the app. Before heading outdoors, see temperature and conditions at your location. Combined with the compass and GPS, you get a complete outdoor navigation toolkit in one app — direction, position, level, and weather all in your pocket.

Works Offline

The compass and level meter work without internet. The magnetometer sensor operates independently, so you get reliable direction finding even in remote areas with no signal. An offline compass you can trust when it matters most.

Built for Outdoor Adventures
