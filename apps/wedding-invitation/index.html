About This App

Design stunning wedding invitations in minutes with the most complete invitation card maker for Android. Choose from 100+ professionally designed templates — floral, modern, vintage, elegant, rustic, and minimalist styles — then customize every element to match your wedding theme perfectly. Your wedding invitation maker features a full multi-layer card editor. Add couple names, wedding date, venue, and RSVP details with our easy text editor. Pick from dozens of wedding-appropriate fonts, adjust colors, and place decorative elements like floral frames and elegant dividers directly onto your card design. Create personalized invitations by adding your own photos. The photo and name card editor makes it effortless to design unique invitations that reflect your personal style — no design experience required. Send your invitations digitally the moment they're ready. Export finished cards as PNG, JPEG, or PDF with a single tap, then share them instantly via any messaging app. Works completely offline so you can design your wedding cards anywhere, anytime. ✦ Features • 100+ wedding invitation templates — floral, vintage, modern, elegant • Multi-layer card editor with full element control • Add couple names, dates, venue, and custom text • Font picker with wedding-appropriate selections • Color customization for text, backgrounds, and accents • Photo integration for personalized card designs • Decorative elements: frames, flowers, dividers • Export as PNG, JPEG, or PDF • Instant digital sharing with guests • Full offline support — no internet needed Whether you are planning a big ceremony or a small gathering, this invite maker handles every detail. Design beautiful greeting cards, announcement cards, and digital invitations for every occasion — weddings, engagements, anniversaries, and birthday parties.
