About

Tanning UV Index - Sun Tracker

iStack
Tanning UV Index - Sun Tracker is an iPhone app for planning sun exposure around live UV index, Fitzpatrick skin type, SPF level, sunburn risk timing, vitamin D estimates, 7-day UV forecasts, sunscreen reapplication reminders, and session history. It is a planning tool for beach days, pool sessions, vacations, and everyday outdoor routines; it is not a medical device or a guarantee that tanning is safe.
What the app helps with
Use the app when you want a UV index tanning timer instead of a plain weather number. Set your skin type, SPF, and session goal, then use the countdown-style guidance to decide when to check your skin, reapply sunscreen, seek shade, or stop a session.
UV, SPF, and sunburn planning
The app combines live UV index and a 7-day forecast with Fitzpatrick skin type inputs and SPF reminders. This helps users compare lower-risk and higher-risk outdoor windows before stepping into the sun.
Vitamin D and session history
Estimated vitamin D exposure and session logs help users review patterns over time. These estimates are informational only and should not replace professional medical guidance, sunscreen label directions, or public-health advice about UV exposure.
U.S. and Mexico search fit
The page is written for English and Spanish search intent around UV index, sunburn timer, SPF reminder, indice UV, bronceado seguro, protector solar, vitamina D, and quemaduras solares. The App Store listing is in English, so Spanish-search users should review the app before relying on it.

Frequently asked questions

What does Tanning UV Index - Sun Tracker do?
Tanning UV Index - Sun Tracker helps users plan sun exposure with live UV index, Fitzpatrick skin type, SPF level, sunburn risk timing, vitamin D estimates, SPF reminders, a 7-day forecast, and session history.
Is this a UV index app or a tanning timer app?
It is both. The app uses UV index data as the input, then turns it into a more practical tanning and sun-exposure timer based on skin type, SPF, and session goals.
Can it help users in the U.S. and Mexico?
Yes. The app is built for location-based UV planning, which is useful for U.S. and Mexico beach, pool, vacation, and daily outdoor sun exposure. Spanish-search users may find it through terms like indice UV, bronceado seguro, protector solar, and quemaduras solares.
Can I use it for beach or summer trip planning?
Yes. The app is designed for beach days, pool sessions, vacations, and summer outdoor planning. Check the UV forecast before going out, then use SPF, Fitzpatrick skin type, and timer reminders as guidance while you follow sunscreen labels and local weather alerts.
How should users treat UV and tanning guidance?
Use UV, SPF, tanning, sunburn, and vitamin D estimates as planning guidance only. UV exposure can still damage skin, and users should follow public-health advice, sunscreen label directions, and professional medical guidance for personal risk.
Does the app replace medical advice?
No. Tanning UV Index - Sun Tracker provides guidance based on UV data and personal inputs. It is not a medical device and is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
Is Tanning UV Index - Sun Tracker free?
Yes. The app is free to download on the App Store and offers optional premium access through Apple in-app purchases or subscriptions.
