How to use cool fonts on Instagram with Font Generator

Updated April 19, 2026 · About Font Generator

Font Generator creates stylish, decorative text that you can copy and paste into any app — Instagram bios, WhatsApp status, Twitter, or anywhere else. No special keyboard or app permissions needed.

Step-by-step guide

  1. 1

    Download Font Generator

    Install Font Generator from Google Play — it's free. No account required. Search "Font Generator iStack" to find the right app.

  2. 2

    Type your text

    Open the app and tap the text field at the top. Type the word, name, or phrase you want to style — for example your Instagram username or a bio line.

  3. 3

    Browse and pick a font style

    Scroll through the list of generated styles. Each line shows your text in a different decorative Unicode font — bold, italic, cursive, bubble letters, and more. Tap any style to select it.

  4. 4

    Copy and paste

    Tap the Copy button. Then open Instagram (or any other app), tap into the bio or caption field, and paste. The styled text works on any platform that supports Unicode.

Frequently asked questions

Why does Font Generator work on Instagram without a special keyboard?
▾
Font Generator uses Unicode characters that look like different fonts but are actually standard text symbols. Any app that accepts text input will display them — no special permissions needed.
Will the fonts look the same on every phone?
▾
The characters are Unicode standard, so they display on all modern devices. Older devices may show some characters as boxes if they lack the required Unicode support.
Does Font Generator work for WhatsApp?
▾
Yes. Copy the styled text from Font Generator and paste it into any WhatsApp message, status, or display name.
